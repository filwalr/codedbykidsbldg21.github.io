What is Bullying?

Bullying is saying or doing harmful things to someone with the intent of making them uncomfortable. Such as physical and verbal abuse, sexual harassment, slurs, excluding someone from groups, stalking someone on social media

How does this issue affect society?

Kids who are bullied are more likely to have anxiety and depression. It can also affect their academic behavior.

How can you raise awareness?

Talk about it with students, help them understand what bullying is and what it can do to others. And help the students to do what they love

Statistics

  • Over 14 percent of high school students have considered suicide, and almost 7 percent have attempted it
  • More than 77% of people are bullied verbally, mentally, and physically.
  • More than 160,000 people miss school because of bullying
